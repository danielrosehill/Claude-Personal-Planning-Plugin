# Personal Planning Workspace — Personal Development variant

## Purpose

General personal-development workspace — skills, habits, learning goals, progress tracking.

## Workspace Facts

<!-- Populated by /personal-planning:new-workspace — top skill areas / goals -->

## Primitives

- `/personal-planning:log-entry` — log a progress entry (writes to `progress/`)
- `/personal-planning:review-progress` — review progress against goals
- `/personal-planning:set-goal` — set or update a development goal

## Structure

- `progress/` — dated progress entries
- `planning/` — plans and roadmaps
- `resources/` — learning resources (books, courses, links)
- `prompts/` — reusable coaching prompts
- `goals/` — active goals
- `outputs/` — reviews and retrospectives

## Rules

- Favour measurable goals (reps, hours, deliverables) where possible.
- Retrospect weekly; re-plan monthly; re-evaluate priorities quarterly.
